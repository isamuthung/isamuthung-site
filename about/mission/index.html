Q: What Will I Do At Stanford?

A: After reviewing Stanford’s undergraduate programs and realizing that if I select a pre-defined track I’d be caught up in many classes that don't align with my interests and goals, I’ve been leaning toward crafting an Individually Designed Major (IDM) that integrates data analytics, AI research, systems thinking, and social entrepreneurship that would grant me the autonomy to take the classes that spark my curiosity, collaborate with faculty and peers that inspire me, and take real action towards bringing value into the world.

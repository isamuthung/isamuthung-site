Courage to Create

Courage to Create, a 32-episode podcast I produced over the course of a year, documented my journey through project-based learning during my junior year at High Tech High Mesa. Each episode offered candid reflections on everything from school projects and internships to broader questions about education and personal growth. More than just updates, I shared personal stories, aspirations, and insights—using the podcast as a space to explore how curiosity and reflection serve as catalysts for growth.

what is my vision for education?

I aspire for schools be a marketplace of learning where curiosity drives process, success is measured by the questions students ask, and young people are left with the skills, confidence, and intrinsic motivation to shape fulfilling lives for themselves.

what is my vision for my life?

I aspire to live an unbounded life immersed in nature, surrounded by good-hearted people, and filled with world-class experiences, and eventually I seek to transform educational systems to allow for young people, everywhere to do the same.

What is my vision for business?

I aspire to develop tools to create digital assets and product ecosystems, discover the best practices to organize human capital in a world of AI, and democratize the process of scaling a profitable and sustainable business.
